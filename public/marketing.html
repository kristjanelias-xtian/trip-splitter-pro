How it works

Up and running in minutes

For Trips Multi-day

  1. 1

    Create your trip

    Name, dates, and base currency. Share the link with your group — no sign-up needed.

  2. 2

    Add participants

    Individuals or families — Spl1t handles both. Invite by email or just share the link.

  3. 3

    Log expenses as you go

    Scan receipts with AI, or enter amounts manually. Multi-currency is automatic.

  4. 4

    Settle up

    Optimized transfers — the fewest payments to square everyone up. Bank details included.

  1. 1

    Scan the receipt

    Take a photo right from the app. AI extracts merchant, items, and totals in seconds.

  2. 2

    Add your friends

    Type names and tap to assign items. A group is created automatically from the scan.

  3. 3

    Send reminders

    One tap sends payment reminders with a full receipt breakdown and bank details.
